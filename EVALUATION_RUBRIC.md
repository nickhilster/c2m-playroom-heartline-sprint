# Evaluation Rubric

This shared rubric provides a consistent scoring matrix for Playroom contribution review.

## Scoring Criteria (1-5)
- Experience Clarity: Is the core interaction model clear and memorable?
- Playroom Fit: Does the concept extend Playroom with a distinct, complementary lane?
- Emotional Impact: Does the experience deliver a meaningful emotional or affective response?
- Accessibility & Safety: Are accessibility, consent, and low-risk design considerations clearly handled?
- Prototype Feasibility: Can the MVP be implemented with a focused, realistic scope?
- Share & Replay Potential: Does the design include a meaningful seedable artifact or replay/share object?

## Total
- Maximum score: 30 points
